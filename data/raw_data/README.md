# Raw data for "Climate Change Impacts on Portugal's Fishing Industry"

This subdirectory contains all of the raw data used in this analysis. 

These are the datasets used for exploratory data analysis:

- `1903-1949_Landings.csv`: ICES Historical Landings 1903-1949. Obtained from [ICES CIEM](https://www.ices.dk/data/dataset-collections/Pages/Fish-catch-and-stock-assessment.aspx).
- `	ICES_1950-2010.csv`: Historical Nominal Catches 1950-2010. Obtained from [ICES CIEM](https://www.ices.dk/data/dataset-collections/Pages/Fish-catch-and-stock-assessment.aspx).
- `ICESCatchDataset2006-2023.csv`: Official Nominal Catches 2006-2023. Obtained from [ICES CIEM](https://www.ices.dk/data/dataset-collections/Pages/Fish-catch-and-stock-assessment.aspx).
- `atlantic_ocean_temp.csv`: North-East Atlantic Ocean Sea Surface Temperature from 1859-2024. Obtained from the [European Environment Agency](https://www.eea.europa.eu/en/analysis/indicators/european-sea-surface-temperature?activeAccordion=309c5ef9-de09-4759-bc02-802370dfa366).
- `	ASFIS_sp_2025.csv`: Includes FAO species and their common, scientific, and FAO code names. Obtained from the [Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations](https://www.fao.org/fishery/en/collection/asfis).
- `portugal_fishery_stats.csv`: Includes Portugal's annual national fish price (euros/kg) from 2011-2024. Obtained from text to data conversion from [Statistics Portugal's](https://www.ine.pt/xportal/xmain?xpid=INE&xpgid=ine_pesquisa&frm_accao=PESQUISAR&frm_show_page_num=1&frm_modo_pesquisa=PESQUISA_SIMPLES&frm_texto=Anu%C3%A1rio+estat%C3%ADstico+de+Portugal&frm_modo_texto=MODO_TEXTO_ALL&frm_data_ini=&frm_data_fim=&frm_tema=QUALQUER_TEMA&frm_area=o_ine_area_Publicacoes&xlang=en) Annual Fishery Statistics Report.
- `countries_iso3166b.csv`: Includes countries and their ISO codes. Obtained from BDILL on [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/wbdill/country-codes-iso-3166).
