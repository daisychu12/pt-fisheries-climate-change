# Processed data for "Climate Change Impacts on Portugal's Fishing Industry"

This subdirectory contains all of the raw data used in this analysis. 

These are the datasets used for final visualizations and analysis with the 5 species specified below being the top 5 most fished species from 1997 to 2023:
- `	pt_annual_landings.csv`: annual sum of landings of all species catches by Portugal from 1997 to 2023
- `clim_and_cost.csv`: ocean temperature and annual price of fish (euros/kg)
- `sardine.csv`: Portugal's sardine catches from 1997 to 2023 along with ocean temperature and price of fish
- `horse_mack.csv`: Portugal's Atlantic horse mackerel catches from 1997 to 2023 along with ocean temperature and price of fish
- `common_oct.csv`: Portugal's common octopus catches from 1997 to 2023 along with ocean temperature and price of fish
- `bl_jack.csv`: Portugal's blue jack catches from 1997 to 2023 along with ocean temperature and price of fish
- `atl_cod.csv`: Portugal's Atlantic cod catches from 1997 to 2023 along with ocean temperature and price of fish
